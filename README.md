# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee data to identify critical workforce trends and attrition patterns using Power BI. The goal is to provide HR leadership with actionable insights to improve employee retention and optimize workforce demographics.

---

## Tools Used
* **Business Intelligence:** Power BI
* **Data Processing:** Microsoft Excel
* **Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## Dataset Overview
The analysis is built upon a dataset containing records for **1,000 employees**, capturing key data points such as:
* HR Attributes (Department, Role, Education, Job Satisfaction, Overtime)
* Demographic Info (Age, Gender)
* Compensation (Salary Information)
* Retention Metrics (Attrition Status, Years at Company)

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks five core high-level metrics:
* **Total Employees:** Total workforce headcount ($1,000$)
* **Attrition Count:** Number of employees who have left the company ($246$)
* **Attrition Rate:** Percentage of workforce turnover ($24.6\%$)
* **Average Salary:** Mean employee compensation ($7,849$)
* **Average Age:** Mean age of the workforce ($39.9\text{ Years}$)

---

## Dashboard Features & Visualizations
* **Attrition by Department:** Identifies turnover counts across business units.
* **Gender Distribution:** A breakdown of total workforce headcount by gender.
* **Age Group Analysis:** Tracks employee distributions across different age brackets.
* **Salary Analysis:** Explores average salary ranges by department.
* **Job Satisfaction Analysis:** Correlates employee satisfaction ratings with attrition counts.
* **Years at Company Trend:** A timeline tracking attrition patterns relative to employee tenure.

---

## Key Business Insights
* **Targeted Department Attrition:** PINPOINTS specific business units experiencing disproportionately high attrition rates.
* **Workforce Demographics:** Analyzes gender and age distributions to monitor organizational diversity.
* **Compensation Mapping:** Understands salary distributions to ensure market competitiveness.
* **Retention Patterns:** Explores how job satisfaction and overtime impact overall employee turnover.
